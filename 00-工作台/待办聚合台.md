# ✅ 待办聚合台

> 聚合 00-工作台 和 01-造价核心 中的所有未完成任务

## 未完成任务

`dataview
TASK
FROM "00-工作台" OR "01-造价核心"
WHERE !completed
SORT created DESC
`

## 本周完成

`dataview
TASK
FROM "00-工作台" OR "01-造价核心"
WHERE completed
SORT completed DESC
LIMIT 30
`

## 按来源统计

`dataview
TABLE length(rows) as "待办数"
FROM "00-工作台" OR "01-造价核心"
WHERE file.tasks
FLATTEN file.tasks as t
WHERE !t.completed
GROUP BY file.folder as "来源"
SORT length(rows) DESC
`
